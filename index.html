About Me






Hello!

I'm currently a Master's student at Brown University majoring in Computer Science. Previously, I got my undergraduate degree from the University of California, Santa Cruz, majoring in Computer Science as well. I love programming because designing and writing computer programs lets me use my creativity and problem-solving skills. I've gained a lot of practical experience during college and graduate school. Therefore, I'm intimately familiar with several coding languages including Java, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, Git, Pytorch, and Tensorflow.

Outside of class, I've worked on two group projects during the summer vacation and published two papers about them. They are: Visualizing large-scale high-dimensional data via hierarchical embedding of KNN graphs, and Facial expression recognition based on multi branch structure.

I've also learned a lot about clustered data processing, especially Hadoop, MapReduce, Hive, ZooKeeper, Yarn, HiveSQL, and I was able to set up a workable Hadoop Cluster on my own personal computer.
